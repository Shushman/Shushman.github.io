Shushman Choudhury

I am an applied researcher with deep expertise across Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques such as foundation models, decision-making, and multi-agent systems, as well as domains including global-scale geospatial intelligence, autonomous mobility networks, and robotics.

Currently, I am an AI Software Engineer at Google Research. I develop foundational models and applications that advance our understanding of the built environment. I have worked on both impactful research and cross-functional product solutions with Google Maps, Sustainability, Ads, and other teams.

I was previously Research Lead at Lacuna Technologies, where I developed intelligent digital platforms to optimize urban transportation. I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University. My thesis studied multi-agent decision-making for large-scale autonomous systems and included award-winning research covered by the BBC, Venture Beat, and IEEE Spectrum.
